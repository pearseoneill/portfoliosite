About Me!

Hi! I'm Pearse and I'm a 20 year old Graphic Designer based in Belfast, Ireland. I currently study Interaction Design at the Belfast School of Art. I have always had a keen interest in branding and identity design but through my time as an IxD student I have developed a love for creating illustrations and icons therefore Adobe Illustrator is one of my favourite pieces of software to use.

In my spare time I love to travel and experience the outdoors, I recently travelled New Zealand (pictured above). Photography is a great interest of mine and needless to say I do a lot of instagramming.
