'Not Ours' Zine

The Brief

'Not Ours' is a zine I made as a side project during a summer break. I wanted to create a piece of work venting my frustrations at the animal agriculture industry. I wanted to use hard hitting imagery in the form of collage and print.

My Approach

Already having a good idea of how I wanted this project to look like i began sketching out my ideas. I had the strong colour scheme of black and red in my head and used thuis in my sketches to conceptualise pages in the zine.

Whilst sketching out my ideas I came up with the phrase 'Not Ours' relating to animals and the planet not being 'ours'as humans to exploit and destroy. I wanted this to be the title of the zine.

After planning out my ideas I started experimenting with colllage, I would scan pages from old books, pictures of animals butchers etc and manipulate them to create jarring images that woudl hit home. I also worked on Illustrator to create some of my ideas.

When experimenting with different ways to create the 'Not Ours' cover I used wooden letter stamps with red ink to type out the title creating a sort of bloddy effect. I loved how this turned out. I also decided at this point that I wanted the 'Not Ours' text throughout the zine so I scanned in my letter stamped type and placed it on each spread in the zine to make a full sentence relating to the collage on that spread.

Final Outcome

Once I got the entire zine put together on screen, I got it printed and bound. I was so hapy with how it turned. I loved being able to use my passion for design tomake art that spoke for something I cared about.
